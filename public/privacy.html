Local image processing

Web Image Editor processes imported images in your browser. The app does not upload your image files to an application server for editing, rendering, storage, or download.

  • Opening, painting, cropping, resizing, object placement, and exports happen on your device.
  • Downloaded files are generated in the browser from the visible canvas.
  • Closing or refreshing the page may remove unsaved editor state.

OpenMoji stickers

Sticker search uses a local OpenMoji index in the browser. When you add a sticker, the selected OpenMoji SVG is loaded from the jsDelivr CDN. OpenMoji graphics are licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.

OpenMoji stickers may be included in your exported image. You are responsible for making sure your final image and how you use or share it complies with the OpenMoji license and any rights in your own images.

Data we do not collect

  • The app does not maintain its own image upload database.
  • The app does not intentionally collect edited image contents.
  • The app does not sell personal information.
